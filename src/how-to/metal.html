What to do with: Metal

Metal is often something you instinctively put in the recycle whole, but sometimes have special rules! Check below before proceeding.
Metal Cans
Alluminum and Tin cans are perfectly recycleable, but if they do not contain a metal lid larger than 3’, make sure to not include the lid (this does not apply to the can or tin openers on most bottles, should those fall off).
Scrap Metal
Scrap metal needs to fit specific dimensions in order to be recycled. Scrap metal put in your blue recycling bin should be at least 3’ in one direction, but no longer than 2 feet. If your metal is too large, find a local scrap mill or US designated transfer station for the metal.
